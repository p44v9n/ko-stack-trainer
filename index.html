Resources

To learn more about card magic using memorised stacks, please see the following:

  • Memories Are Made Of This, a free ebook by Simon Aronson that gives an introduction to magic with memorised decks
  • Mnemonica by Juan Tamariz, which reveals the workings of the Mnemonica stack, teaches how to memorise any stack forever in three hours, and has over a hundred tricks and routines
  • Bound To Please by Simon Aronson
  • Memorandum by Woody Aragón, soon to be released in English
  • This blogpost about the benefits of Patrick Redford's stack, which is explained in his new book, Temporarily Out Of Order
